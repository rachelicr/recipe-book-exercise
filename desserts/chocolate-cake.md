# Chocolate Cake Recipe

## Ingredients
- 200g (1 cup) granulated sugar
- 175g (3/4 cup) all-purpose flour
- 75g (3/4 cup) unsweetened cocoa powder
- 1½ tsp baking powder
- 1½ tsp baking soda
- 1 tsp salt
- 2 large eggs
- 240ml (1 cup) milk
- 120ml (1/2 cup) vegetable oil
- 2 tsp vanilla extract
- 240ml (1 cup) boiling water

## Instructions
- Preheat the oven to 180°C (160°C fan)/350°F/Gas Mark 4. 
- Grease and flour a 23cm (9-inch) round cake pan or line with baking parchment.
- Mix dry ingredients: In a large bowl, sift together flour, cocoa powder, baking powder, baking soda, and salt. Stir in the sugar.
- Combine wet ingredients: In another bowl, whisk together eggs, milk, oil, and vanilla extract until smooth.
- Combine wet and dry mixes: Gradually add the wet mixture to the dry ingredients and mix until just combined.
- Add boiling water: Carefully stir in the boiling water. The batter will be thin, but this ensures a moist cake.
- Bake: Pour batter into the prepared pan and bake for 30–35 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
- Cool: Allow the cake to cool in the pan for 10 minutes, then transfer to a wire rack to cool completely before frosting or serving.
